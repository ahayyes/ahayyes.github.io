# April Hayes

I am a microbiologist interested in the ecology and evolution of microbial communities in response to stressors, specifically how this relates to the development and spread of antimicrobial resistance. Some of my previous work has shown how antibiotics, and other non-antibiotic pharmaceuticals (like painkillers or diabetes medications) can affect microbial communities. However, my other interests involve how communities are protective (or not) for the species within them, and how the presence of a community alters population responses.
